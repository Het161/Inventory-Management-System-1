## ‚ú® Features

### üì¶ Inventory Management
- **Product Catalog**: Track products with SKU, category, price, and stock levels
- **Multi-Category Organization**: Organize products into customizable categories
- **Low Stock Alerts**: Automated notifications when inventory falls below minimum thresholds
- **Stock Movement Tracking**: Record all Stock In and Stock Out transactions
- **Complete Audit Trail**: Transaction history with user attribution and timestamps
- **Batch Operations**: Update multiple products simultaneously
- **Product Search**: Quick product lookup by name, SKU, or category

### üè≠ Warehouse & Outlet Management
- **Multiple Locations**: Manage unlimited warehouses and retail outlets
- **Location-Specific Inventory**: Track stock levels at each location separately
- **Stock Transfers**: Transfer inventory between locations with full tracking
- **Real-time Availability**: View stock availability across all locations instantly
- **Location Analytics**: Performance metrics for each warehouse/outlet

### üë• Customer & Staff Management
- **Customer Database**: Comprehensive customer information and purchase history
- **Staff Management**: Employee records with role-based permissions
- **User Activity Logs**: Complete audit trails of all user actions
- **Access Control**: Granular permissions for different user roles (Admin, Manager, Staff)
- **Customer Insights**: Track customer purchase patterns and preferences

### üìä Analytics & Reporting
- **Revenue Analysis**: Track revenue, profit, and expenses over time
- **Sales Trends**: Visualize sales patterns with interactive charts
- **Category Performance**: Analyze performance by product category
- **Custom Date Ranges**: Filter reports for specific time periods
- **Export Capabilities**: Download reports in CSV and PDF formats
- **Real-time Dashboards**: Live KPI updates on the main dashboard
- **Predictive Analytics**: AI-powered forecasting and insights

### ü§ñ AI Agents (Advanced Features)
- **Inventory Optimization**: Smart suggestions for stock level management
- **Expense Anomaly Detection**: Identify unusual spending patterns automatically
- **Automated Calling Agents**: Schedule automated calls to suppliers
- **Predictive Stock Recommendations**: AI-driven reorder point suggestions
- **Demand Forecasting**: Predict future inventory needs based on historical data

### üîç Advanced Search & Filtering
- **Global Search**: Search across products, customers, and transactions from anywhere
- **Advanced Filters**: Filter by category, status, date range, and more
- **Sort Capabilities**: Sort results by name, price, stock, date, etc.
- **Quick Actions**: Perform actions directly from search results

### üé® User Experience
- **Modern UI**: Clean, intuitive interface built with Tailwind CSS
